
# Maryland Transit Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 13, 2026

## Introduction

Welcome to **Maryland Transit**. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect certain information to provide a better user experience and to support the development of the App.

### A. Device Identifiers and Tracking

If you grant permission through the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt, we may collect:

* **Advertising Identifier (IDFA on iOS):** This identifier is used to show personalized advertisements and help us understand the effectiveness of our marketing efforts.

### B. App Usage Data

We may collect information about how you interact with the App, such as:

* Routes and stops searched or viewed.
* Application performance data (e.g., crash reports).

### C. Locally Stored Data

To provide features like "Favorites," we store your preferred stops and routes locally on your device using `Shared Preferences`. This data remains on your device and is not uploaded to our servers.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

* **To provide and maintain the Service:** Showing real-time transit predictions and route information.
* **To personalize your experience:** Saving your favorite stops for quick access.
* **To show advertisements:** Using third-party services to display relevant ads.
* **To improve our Service:** Analyzing usage patterns to enhance App functionality.

## 3. Third-Party Service Providers

We use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

* **Google AdMob:** Used for displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers to serve personalized ads. Please refer to [Googleâ€™s Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for more details.

## 4. Your Privacy Choices

* **App Tracking Transparency:** You can choose to opt-out of ad tracking by selecting "Ask App Not to Track" when prompted, or by changing your settings in **Settings > Privacy > Tracking**.
* **Local Data:** You can clear your saved favorites at any time within the App settings or by deleting the App.

## 5. Children's Privacy

Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

## 7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
